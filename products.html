Posalux Tools


Product Description


Posalux tools are diamond cutting tools for use in jewelry machines for cutting rings, bangles, and chains. These tools provide a shiny mirror effect on metal surfaces like gold, silver, and platinum. They come in a variety of options, including diamond, MCD (mono), and CVD diamond.

The tools have features such as best quality and finishing, longer endurance and consistent working efficiency, and are available in natural options. They can also be repolished or repaired.

C.N.C Tools


Product Description


CNC tools are cutting tools for use in computer numerical control (CNC) machines for engraving and other design work. They come in MCD and CVD diamond options, with a standard shank size of 6mm and length of 40mm.

These tools offer optimal performance with a spindle speed of 25000 RPM or higher and can be used for engraving and different design shapes such as point, ball, circle, and lining. Special orders can be accommodated.

Product Description


A Diamond Dull Pin is a tool used for creating a matte finish or engraving on jewelry pieces, such as bangles, rings, ball, and para mani. It is available in mono diamond and carbon materials and comes in different shapes with 4, 6, or 8 facets.

The diamond dull pin is an essential tool for adding unique textures and designs to jewelry pieces.

Hammer Tools


Product Description


Hammer tools are a cost-effective solution for jewelry hand engraving. They are easy to use and offer a much lower cost compared to CNC tools or Posalux tools. These tools allow for precise and intricate engraving on jewelry pieces, making them ideal for traditional hand engraving techniques.

Optional Material:- Natural Diamond, Mono Diamond, CVD Diamond

P.C.D Tools


Product Description


PCD (Polycrystalline Diamond) tools are commonly used for making bangle bases because of their durability and long lifespan. They offer high wear resistance and maintain their cutting edge for longer periods compared to traditional cutting tools.

Our company provides a wide range of PCD tools that come in all sizes and shapes, from 2mm to 20mm, including flat, lining, concave, and convex shapes. We can also customize the tools as per the customer's requirements.

Magic Wheel/ Mill Grane Wheel


Product Description


A Magic Wheel/ Mill Grane Wheel is a diamond plated design wheel used for shaping and finishing on bangles and rings. It is available in smooth, medium, and rough options to suit different surface finishes.

The Magic Wheel/ Mill Grane Wheel is easy to use by hand or on a lathe machine, making it a versatile tool for jewelry making and polishing.
